Apparel and Fashion Technology

Industrial sewing and production floors, CAD labs, and textile testing equipment for applied practice.

Automotive Technology

Workshop bays with lifts, diagnostic tools, and dynamometer labs for performance and systems testing.

Computer Technology

High-performance PC labs, server rooms, and networking gear for systems and infrastructure practice.

Culinary Technology

Pilot plants for processing, analytical labs, and equipment for food safety & quality testing.

Information Technology

Data centers, specialized labs for network management, and cybersecurity practice on virtual platforms.

Electrical Technology

Test benches, power supplies, and labs for motor control and power distribution simulation.

Electronics Technology

Workbenches with diagnostic tools, PCB areas, and equipment for circuit testing and prototyping.

Mechanical Technology

Machine shops with CNC machinery, testing labs for fluids, thermodynamics, and manufacturing processes.

Print Media Technology

Industrial printing presses and design suites for professional print production and finishing practice.
